## Networking Strategy

### LinkedIn (Daily — 10 min)
- [ ] Connect with 2-3 new people daily (DS/MLE professionals, recruiters)
- [ ] Comment meaningfully on 2-3 posts daily
- [ ] Post 1-2x per week about your learning journey
- [ ] Share project milestones with screenshots

### Twitter/X (Optional — 10 min)
- [ ] Follow ML researchers and practitioners
- [ ] Share #BuildInPublic updates
- [ ] Engage with #100DaysOfML or #100DaysOfCode

### Events
- [ ] Attend 1 virtual meetup per month minimum
- [ ] Present at a local meetup by Month 10
- [ ] Attend PyData Africa or similar conference
